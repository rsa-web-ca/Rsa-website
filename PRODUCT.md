# Product

## Register

brand

## Platform

web

## Users

Business owners, finance teams, and NRIs in and around Bengaluru looking for a chartered accountant firm for audit, tax, and advisory work. Secondary audience: CA students and audit professionals evaluating the firm as an employer via the Careers page.

## Product Purpose

The public website of R Shivakumar & Associates, Chartered Accountants (est. 1997, Bengaluru). It presents the firm's nine service lines, the industries it serves, and gives visitors two conversion paths: a contact enquiry form and a careers application form. Success is a visitor trusting the firm enough to call, email, or submit a form.

## Positioning

A Bengaluru CA firm with 25+ years of practice depth across audit, tax litigation, and advisory — senior-partner attention, not big-four anonymity.

## Conversion & proof

- Primary CTA: Contact us (enquiry form / phone).
- Secondary CTA: Careers application for candidates.
- The line a visitor remembers: "Rendering audit, tax and advisory services since 1997."
- Belief ladder: (1) this is an established, credentialed firm; (2) they cover my specific need (service pages); (3) they know my industry; (4) reaching them is easy.
- Proof on hand: founding year 1997, ICAI standards references, breadth of nine service lines and ten industry sectors.

## Brand Personality

Assured, precise, established. The voice of a senior partner: plain-spoken, unhurried, and confident without salesmanship.

## Anti-references

- Big-four corporate gloss and stock-photo handshake clichés.
- Startup-SaaS landing page grammar (gradient text, metric hero, playful illustration).
- The outdated 2010s template look of the previous Wix site.

## Design Principles

1. Trust is earned in the details — alignment, contrast, and typography do the persuading.
2. Content unchanged, presentation elevated — every word from the original site is preserved.
3. One clear action per page; forms are short, labelled, and forgiving.
4. Quietly modern — motion and color signal care, never spectacle.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

WCAG AA: 4.5:1 body contrast, visible focus states, keyboard-navigable menus, labelled form fields, prefers-reduced-motion respected.
