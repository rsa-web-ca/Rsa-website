Welcome to the R Shivakumar & Associates journal. This is our first post, and
it is a simple one: **hello world**.

We have been rendering audit, tax and advisory services since 1997, and over
that time we have answered the same good questions many times over — about a
new filing requirement, a change in the tax code, or what a particular notice
from the department actually means. This space is where we intend to write some
of those answers down.

## What you can expect

- **Plain-spoken notes** on changes in audit, taxation and compliance.
- **Practical explainers** for business owners and finance teams.
- The occasional reflection on the practice of chartered accountancy itself.

> Trust is earned in the details. We will try to keep these posts precise,
> useful, and free of jargon wherever we can.

Thank you for reading. If there is a topic you would like us to cover, we would
be glad to hear from you.
